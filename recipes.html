Gajar Halwa

It is a traditional Indian dessert made from grated carrots, cooked slowly with milk, sugar, and ghee. It's flavored with cardamom and saffron, resulting in a rich, creamy, and aromatic sweet treat. Garnished with nuts, it's enjoyed warm or cold, making it a beloved dessert during festivals and special occasions in India.
