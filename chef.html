  • Vikas Khanna

    Vikas Khanna is a world-renowned chef, restaurateur, cookbook author, and humanitarian. Born in Amritsar, India, Khanna discovered his passion for cooking at an early age, influenced by his grandmother's traditional Indian recipes. He trained at the esteemed Culinary Institute of America and has since become a prominent figure in the culinary world, known for his innovative approach to Indian cuisine.
