Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for caramel)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for pudding)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Caramel:
    • Heat 1/2 cup of granulated sugar in a heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat.
    • Stir occasionally until the sugar melts and turns into a golden-brown caramel syrup. Be careful not to burn the sugar.
    • Once the caramel is ready, pour it into the bottom of a pudding mold or individual ramekins. Tilt the mold or ramekins to evenly coat the bottom with caramel. Let it cool and harden.
  2. Prepare Pudding Mixture:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and 1/2 cup of granulated sugar until well combined.
    • Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat until it just starts to simmer. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
    • Gradually pour the warm milk into the egg-sugar mixture, stirring continuously.
    • Add vanilla extract and mix well.
  3. Strain and Pour:
    • Strain the pudding mixture through a fine sieve to remove any lumps.
    • Pour the strained mixture over the cooled caramel in the pudding mold or ramekins.
  4. Bake:
    • Place the pudding mold or ramekins in a baking dish filled with hot water, creating a water bath (bain-marie).
    • Carefully transfer the baking dish to the preheated oven.
    • Bake for about 45-50 minutes, or until the pudding is set around the edges but slightly wobbly in the center.
  5. Chill and Serve:
    • Once baked, remove the pudding from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
    • Then, refrigerate the pudding for at least 2-3 hours or until thoroughly chilled and set.
    • To serve, run a knife around the edges of the pudding mold or ramekins to loosen the pudding. Invert onto a serving plate to unmold, allowing the caramel to drizzle over the pudding.
    • Slice and serve the caramel pudding cold as a delicious dessert.
